ABOUT US

KITE Sacco Ltd. was registered in 1977 under the Co-operative Societies Act, Cap 490 laws of Kenya, to provide members with an avenue for savings and affordable loans for advancement of their economic wellbeing and became operational in 1978.

It is occupying a rental office at Re-Insurance Plaza (Wedco Center) ground floor Kisumu. It has a Front Office Section which offers banking services to members of the Society with effect from 29 th April, 1997. The Sacco has since open its bond to allow anybody to be a member.
